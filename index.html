Quantum’s World
What is it you see? 1. Runner 2. Ball 3. House 4. Skier 5. Car 6. Plate 7. Wall 8. Brick
Game 1 (The quantum world where  nothingness rules and the classical world where somethingness appears to rule)The game focuses on the perceptual differences between the world of the very small and the world of the large, our world. You begin in  the magnified quantum world and increase the resolution until you are again fully in the classical world.The game allows you to guess what you are seeing at each level the sooner you guess correctly  the more  you will come to perceive the nature of the world upon which your  classical world world rests.
